Voting & Approval Logic

When is an anime officially "Approved"?

A

Unanimous (Current)

Every single member in the group must cast a vote for the anime to be approved.

Strict - good for very small close groups.
B

Simple Majority

Anime is approved when >50% of the members have voted.

Faster - good for larger or active groups.
C

Configurable Threshold

Group creator sets a specific number of votes needed (e.g. "Needs 3 votes").

Flexible - adaptable to any group size.
