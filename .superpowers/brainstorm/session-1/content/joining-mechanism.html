Group Management & Joining

How do users find each other and start a shared catalog?

A

Invite Links

Group creator shares a unique URL. Anyone with the link can join the group instantly.

Share this link: animesrd.com/join/xyz-789
B

Admin Management

Group creator adds members manually by their email address or username.
